# American Express

> American Express is a globally integrated payments company and card network offering charge and credit cards, merchant acquiring, corporate payments, travel, and Membership Rewards loyalty. Its Amex for Developers platform (developer.americanexpress.com) publishes a partner-oriented catalog of REST APIs spanning tokenization, fraud prevention, PSD2 open banking, corporate expense management, points redemption, bank-transfer payment initiation, and network loyalty. Access is onboarding-gated for registered issuers, merchants, acquirers, and partners — there is no open self-service and no public OpenAPI. APIs are secured with mutual TLS, HMAC request signing, and OAuth 2.0.

## APIs

- [American Express Token Service (AETS) API](https://developer.americanexpress.com/products/amex-token-service/overview): Tokenize cardholder data with EMVCo-style network tokens for secure online and card-on-file transactions.
- [Enhanced Authorization (EA)](https://developer.americanexpress.com/products/enhanced-authorization-v2/resources#readme): Fraud-prevention data enrichment passing extra transaction elements at authorization time for real-time risk decisions.
- [Account and Transaction API](https://developer.americanexpress.com/products/account-and-transaction-api-public/overview): PSD2 AISP access to cardholder account and transaction data with consent.
- [Confirmation of Funds PSD2 API](https://developer.americanexpress.com/products/confirmation-of-funds/overview): PSD2 CBPII funds-availability check before completing a transaction.
- [@ Work B2B API](https://developer.americanexpress.com/products/at-work-apis-public/overview): Corporate card account, statement, transaction, and expense-management services.
- [Card On-Demand](https://developer.americanexpress.com/products/nextgen-card-on-demand/resources): Instant digital card provisioning for issuing partners.
- [Pay with Points](https://developer.americanexpress.com/products/global-pay-with-points/resources#readme): Global points redemption at checkout with participating merchants.
- [API-Based Payment Platform (Pay with Bank transfer)](https://developer.americanexpress.com/products/pay-with-bank-transfer-public/overview): PSD2 PISP account-to-account bank-transfer payment initiation.
- [Payment Account Reference (PAR)](https://developer.americanexpress.com/products/payment-account-reference-public/overview): EMVCo PAR linking PANs and network tokens across payment formats.
- [Smart Offer Partnership](https://developer.americanexpress.com/products/smart-offer-partnerships-public/overview): Card-linked offer and loyalty data access via API and SFTP.
- [AMEX Account Connect](https://developer.americanexpress.com/products/nextgen-amex-account-connect/overview): Card-on-file linking using American Express login credentials.
- [Network Loyalty Platform (R42)](https://developer.americanexpress.com/products/network-loyalty/overview): Real-time APIs for issuers to manage elite-tier benefits and points transfers.
